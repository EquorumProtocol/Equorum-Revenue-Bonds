# Equorum Revenue Bonds Protocol

On-chain revenue sharing for DeFi protocols on Arbitrum

## Overview

Revenue Bonds Protocol enables DeFi protocols to share revenue with their community through tokenized revenue bonds.

**Live on Arbitrum One:** [Factory Contract](https://arbiscan.io/address/0x8afA0318363FfBc29Cc28B3C98d9139C08Af737b)
